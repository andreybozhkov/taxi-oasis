About Us

Our Fleet

Are you travelling alone, with your partner or in a group? Do you have a small backpack or a lot of luggage? Are you travelling with a pet? Do you want to try out a place you have never been to before or need guidance about what to do and where to go? We have the right vehicle for every situation in order to meet your needs and ensure you have a smooth and comfortable journey. Our experienced local drivers will be happy to help you in any way they can.

Our Base

We are based in Tsarevo, Bulgaria - a beautiful Southern Bulgarian Black Sea coastal city with a rich history and much to show to the world. We are well-versed in all the local spots and can also pick you up from the airports as far as Varna, Sofia, Burgas as well as Istanbul and drive you back at the end of your stay. Make sure to call us in advance if you are planning to travel from further away.
