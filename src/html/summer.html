Our Collection of All-Time Favourite Summer Ideas

Visit the Old Town of Sozopol

Take a walk around the old town of Sozopol, which is famous for its many artists, musicians and various performers. You will be amazed by the fusion of old architecture and modern venues.

Published by Taxi Oasis on 3rd August 2019.

Take a Ride on a Water Jet

Get your adrenaline levels up by taking a ride on a water jet! This is a fantastic way to experience the rush of the sea wind and water. A skilled instructor will accompany you for safety.

Published by Taxi Oasis on 1st August 2019.

Visit the Balchik Gardens

Especially at the start of the summer, when all the flowers are in their full bloom, it is a great idea to visit the Balchik Gardens. You will be amased by the beauty of all the botanical arrangements!

Published by Taxi Oasis on 1st August 2019.

Take a Boat Tour

You know the view from the beach onto the endless blue. How about taking a look from the sea to the coast? You would be amazed by the beautiful view of the coastline. Enjoy the sunrise or the sunset from a boat.

Published by Taxi Oasis on 28th July 2019.

Visit a Water Park

Skip the sand for a day and take a slide on the wild side! Enjoy the water rides, entertainment programmes and pool bars at one of the water parks. It's fun for both kids and grown-up kids! Kids usually enter at reduced ticket rates.

Published by Taxi Oasis on 21st July 2019.

Attend an Authentic Bulgarian Restaurant

Immerse yourself in the Bulgarian Culture. Many restaurants offer the opportunity to taste delicious local dishes in an authentic atmosphere. You can also watch and even take part of traditional Bulgarian traditions, dances and performances.

Published by Taxi Oasis on 14th July 2019.
